# Deposit with Coinbase

**How to buy and deposit USDC to your Polymarket account using Coinbase.**

Depositing directly to Polymarket from Coinbase is simple and easy. If you need help creating a Coinbase account, see their [guide on coinbase.com](https://help.coinbase.com/en/coinbase/getting-started).

Buy USDC on Coinbase
---
<iframe width="490" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/IlDLdqT8RjU?si=v8iHe20FNqob_Cgr" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>

1. Click **Transfer** on the Coinbase homepage.
2. Click **Deposit Cash** then in **Deposit**, select **Deposit USDC**.
3. Enter an amount & connect a payment method under **Transfer from**.
4. Click **Preview**, review the Order Preview, then click **Deposit cash now**.
5. You'll see a **“Your deposit is pending”** screen. You'll receive a confirmation email from Coinbase when your USDC purchase is successful.

If something went wrong along the way, we recommend reaching to Coinbase support.

Send USDC to your Polymarket account
---
<iframe width="490" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/O6HaKdE9d80?si=NwuCGTzcilUhVQwg" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>
1. Click **Send & Receive**.
2. Under **Asset**, select USD Coin. Note: you may have to search for it.
3. Enter the amount you wish to send (deposit) to Polymarket.
4.  Under **To**, enter your Polymarket deposit address. You can find yours on the Polymarket Deposit page. Then click **Continue**.
Ensure you have copied your address for the Polygon network, as depicted below.

<img className="block h-32 dark:hidden" src="https://polymarket-upload.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/PolygonAddress-light.png" />
<img className="hidden h-32 dark:block" src="https://polymarket-upload.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/PolygonAddress-dark.png" />


5. Change the network to Polygon to ensure you're sending on the right network. Under **Network**, select Polygon.
6. Click **Send Now**. Your deposit will be available to trade on Polymarket in a few minutes.
7. Back on the Polymarket deposit page, Click **Confirm pending deposit**.
